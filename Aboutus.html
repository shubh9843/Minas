VSR Group, National Dyestuff and Chemical was established in 1989 by Shri Vishnu Dayal Agrawal. With more than 30 years of experience in the field, our company has become one of the leading manufacturers and traders of Herbal Gulal, Holi Gulal, Holi Color, Holi Water Colors, industrial paints, and chemicals. Our advanced facility and high-technology machinery enable us to create a wide variety of colors tailored to the unique requirements of our customers.

The Minas Brand Gulal, available in a range of vibrant colors, is highly demanded by customers and is known for its exceptional quality. We pride ourselves on being a quality-centric company, always striving to maintain the highest quality standards in every aspect of our production. Our products are eco-friendly, herbal, non-toxic, and specially formulated to ensure there are no side effects on the skin.

Today, we are scaling new heights in the industry through the persistent efforts and guidance of Mr. Vishnu Dayal Agrawal. His vision inspires us to innovate and continually improve, ensuring that we deliver products which are both safe to use and of the highest quality.
